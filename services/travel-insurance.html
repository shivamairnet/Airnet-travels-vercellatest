Medical Travel Insurance

Covers medical emergencies, hospital visits, and evacuation while traveling, especially in destinations with limited healthcare. Ensures you’re protected in case of illness or injury abroad.

Baggage Insurance

Protects against lost, stolen, or damaged baggage during your trip. Offers reimbursement for your belongings, ensuring peace of mind when traveling.

Emergency Evacuation Insurance

Covers the cost of transportation to a safe location in case of medical emergencies or natural disasters. Ensures you’re protected in extreme situations during your trip.

Accidental Death & Dismemberment Insurance

Provides financial protection for your beneficiaries in case of death or serious injury during your trip. Also covers expenses related to severe injuries sustained while traveling.
